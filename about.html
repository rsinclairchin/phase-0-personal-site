With no clear direction, I drifted over to Santa Rosa Junior College. A few years of taking classes that piqued my interest, and I found myself traveling towards a mathemematics or engineering degree. Engineering was too practical for my taste, and I had fallen head over heels for caluclus, so I carried on in pursuit of graduating with a associates degree in Mathematics. Somewhere in the middle of it all I relocated from SRJC to Napa Community College, where I completed my course.

Since 2013, I've been satisfying to urge to keep learning through online classes. Codeacademy, Udemy, and various online tutorials have served as great resources for me to get a taste for coding. I reached a point where I felt as though I had done every beginner level coding course on the internet, but was not yet ready to delve into more advanced material. I yearned for a stepping stone, and I could not find one. This led me to Dev Bootcamp, where I hope to develop my knowledge further, and also find a place where I belong and settle into a life of building wonderful things with my very own written code.
